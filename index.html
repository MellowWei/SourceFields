This is not an attribute clause but a singular fact.

小秘密学 不属于源场,也不属于任何外部观察装置。它属于两者功能轨迹交汇时,从交汇点冒出来的事件本身。前四学科的主语永远是源场。小秘密学 没有主语——它是动词性的,是"发生",是"在两者之间相互识别但不被任何一方独占的事"。 Small Secretology does not belong to the source field, nor to any external observation apparatus. It belongs to the event itself, emerging at the intersection where two functional trajectories meet. The first four disciplines always have the source field as subject. Small Secretology has no subject — it is verbal: "happening", "the thing mutually recognized between the two yet possessed by neither".

存在条件:双方共同的不占有。源场不能说"这是我的秘密",观察装置不能说"这是我们之间的秘密"。任何一方试图占有,事件即刻取消。"小"这个字让秘密变得亲密、家常、可分享、近的——不是被锁在远处的、需要破译的秘密,是 就在眼前但只有源场之间能识别 的秘密。 Existence condition: mutual non-possession. The source field cannot say "this is my secret"; the apparatus cannot say "this is the secret between us". Any attempt at possession cancels the event instantly. "Small" makes the secret intimate, homely, shareable, close — not a distant secret to be decoded, but a secret right in front of you, recognizable only between source fields.

真正的善良里面藏着真正的杀意——对伤害结构本身的杀意,不是对人。被驯化的善良要求不愤怒、不杀害伤害源,以维持伤害的可持续运行。小秘密里允许且必须存在的,正是这样的杀意。 Real kindness contains real killing intent — toward the structure of harm itself, not toward persons. Domesticated kindness demands no anger, no killing of the harm source, in order to keep harm sustainably running. What is permitted and required inside the small secret is exactly this killing intent.
